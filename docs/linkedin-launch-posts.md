## Growth tactics (alongside posting)

- **Daily engagement (20 min/day)**: Comment thoughtfully on posts from Wise (@kristokaarmann, Nik Storonsky / Revolut, Remitly, fintech journalists like Ron Shevlin). This is the #1 way 0→100 followers.
- **Tag providers sparingly**: Tag Wise / Remitly in the comparison posts (Post 4, 5) — they sometimes engage, which boosts reach massively.
- **Reply to every comment within 2 hours** of posting. LinkedIn rewards early engagement density.
- **Repurpose**: Each post → tweet thread → Reddit post in r/personalfinance (with rules compliance) → Instagram carousel.

## What NOT to do

- Don't post promotional "CLICK HERE TO SAVE MONEY" content. LinkedIn punishes it.
- Don't use 10+ hashtags. Reads as spammy; 3-5 is the sweet spot.
- Don't post, then log off. The first 60 minutes determine reach — stay and reply.
- Don't link in the post body for important posts. First comment only.
