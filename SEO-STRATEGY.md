# SEO Strategy — SendMoneyCompare.com
## Based on Google Search Console Data (March 2026)

---

## Executive Summary

**Current state:** ~400 queries generating impressions, **0 total clicks**. The site is indexed and Google is showing it in results, but positions are mostly 40-95 (pages 4-10), far too low for clicks. However, **a handful of queries already rank on page 1 (positions 1-10)** — these are immediate quick wins.

**Biggest finding:** A massive **business payments** keyword cluster (~200+ impressions) has no dedicated landing page infrastructure. This is a high-value, underserved segment that your single blog guide can't adequately capture.

## 2. Priority Action Plan

### PHASE 1 — Quick Wins (This Week) ⚡
**Goal: Turn page-1 impressions into clicks. Estimated impact: 5-15 clicks/day**

#### 1.1 Optimize titles & meta descriptions for page-1 content

These pages rank positions 1-10 but get 0 clicks. The fix is purely title/meta optimization:

**a) `/guides/global-remittance-trends-2026`**
- Current title: "Global Remittance Trends & Statistics 2026"
- Problem: Generic, doesn't include "India" (your biggest impression keyword)
- **New title:** "Money Transfer & Remittance Statistics 2026 — India, Global Trends & Costs"
- **New meta:** "Latest 2026 data: India received $125B in remittances. Global flows hit $860B. See the full breakdown by country, corridor costs, and digital transfer trends."
- **Target queries:** "money transfer india statistics 2026" (29 imp), "international money transfer statistics 2026" (18 imp)

**b) `/guides/money-transfer-promo-codes-referral-programs`**
- Current title: "Money Transfer Promo Codes & Refer-a-Friend Deals (2026)"
- **New title:** "Money Transfer Promo Codes & First-Time User Deals (March 2026)"
- **New meta:** "Every active promo code, sign-up bonus, and first-time user deal from 14 providers. Special rates for new customers — updated March 2026."
- **Target queries:** "special rates for first-time money transfer users" (14 imp), "best first time user promotions" (3 imp)

**c) `/news/central-bank-rate-week-*`**
- Ensure title includes "This Week" and the current month/year
- **New title pattern:** "Central Bank Rate Decisions This Week (March 2026) — Fed, ECB, BOJ Impact on Transfers"
- **Target queries:** "central bank rate decisions this week march 2026" (8 imp)

#### 1.2 Add FAQ schema targeting exact GSC queries

For each page above, ensure the FAQ schema includes questions matching EXACT GSC query patterns:
- "What are the latest money transfer statistics for India in 2026?"
- "What special rates are available for first-time money transfer users?"
- "How do central bank decisions this week affect transfer rates?"

This is your single biggest content gap. The current single guide at `/guides/business-international-payments-guide` is trying to rank for 30+ distinct queries. You need a **content hub**.

## 6. Key Insights & Risks

### What's working
- **Timely content ranks:** News and trends content (positions 1-8) proves Google values your freshness
- **E-E-A-T signals are strong:** Author pages, corrections log, methodology — this foundation supports ranking improvements
- **Niche authority building:** Provider-specific queries showing Google associates you with money transfers

### What's not working
- **Title/meta CTR:** Page-1 rankings with 0 clicks means titles don't compel clicks
- **Content depth on corridors:** UK→India has 150+ impressions at positions 65-80 — the page exists but isn't competing
- **No business landing pages:** Single biggest gap — 200+ impressions going to a single blog guide

### Risks
- **Business payments = competitive:** Wise Business, OFX, Payoneer, Airwallex all have dedicated pages. Your advantage: **neutral comparison** (you're not a provider)
- **Position volatility:** Page-1 positions for news queries are volatile — maintain freshness
- **Thin content risk:** Business subpages must have genuine depth, not just keyword variations
