## 2. Proven Tactics (Measured ROI)

These tactics have measurable impact from the first 30 days:

### Tier A — Do More Of
| Tactic | Evidence | Expected Impact |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| **Internal cross-linking** | IBAN pages: pos 50-80 → pos 9-22 in 10 days | +30-40 positions per page |
| **Query-echo title rewrites (page 1 only)** | Promo codes: 0→3 clicks after title rewrite | +50-200% CTR on page 1 |
| **Quick-answer boxes on guides** | Remittance trends: 3 clicks at pos 6.0 | Featured snippet capture |
| **Programmatic corridor pages** | 240 pages, 2,046 impr, 8 clicks | Long-tail compound growth |
| **SWIFT code pages** | 5.17% CTR — best of any type | High-intent informational |

### Tier B — Stop or Deprioritize
| Tactic | Evidence | Why |
|--------|----------|-----|
| **Company review pages** | 460 impr, 0 clicks across 19 pages | Branded queries are navigational — users want provider site |
| **Title optimization on pos 20+** | No measured CTR improvement | Doesn't work until page is on page 1-2 |
| **Exchange rate pages** | 1,051 impr, 0 clicks | Google's currency widget captures nearly all clicks |
| **Time-stamped news** | 1,468 impr, 0 clicks | Goes stale fast, 0% CTR. Only publish with evergreen companion |

## 8. What NOT to Do (Learned from Data)

1. **Don't optimize titles for pages ranked 20+** — no measurable CTR effect
2. **Don't invest in company review pages** — branded queries are navigational (0 clicks on 460 impr)
3. **Don't publish time-stamped news without an evergreen companion** — 1,468 impr, 0 clicks
4. **Don't target exchange rate queries** — Google's widget captures nearly all clicks (1,051 impr, 0 clicks)
5. **Don't create thin content pages** — one deep 3,000-word guide outperforms ten 500-word pages
6. **Don't spread effort across all 500 pages** — focus on the 68 pages at position 11-20
