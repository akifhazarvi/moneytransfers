# SEO Strategy 2026 — SendMoneyCompare.com
## 12-Month Strategic Plan (March–December 2026)

---

## Executive Summary

**Site age:** ~2 weeks (launched early March 2026)
**Current state:** 850+ indexed pages, 13 clicks, 6,374 impressions, 0.2% CTR, avg position ~32
**Phase 1 complete:** Title rewrites (11), schema fixes, internal linking expansion, E-E-A-T signals, GEO/AI readiness, business hub (6 pages), all 14 provider reviews, off-site promotion started

**Strategic thesis:** SendMoneyCompare is a neutral comparison platform competing against providers (Wise, Remitly) who rank their own marketing pages, and aggregators (Monito, ExchangeRateIQ, CompareRemit) who have years of domain authority. Our edge: **real-time data from 35+ providers across 80+ corridors, updated every 6 hours**, plus editorial depth that aggregators lack.

**12-month target:** 500+ organic clicks/day, 50K+ monthly impressions, 100+ page-1 keywords

## 1. Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Comparison Sites)
| Competitor | Estimated DR | Key Strength | Key Weakness |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| Monito.com | 60+ | 10yr domain age, 50+ corridors, strong brand | Slow data (not real-time), thin corridor pages |
| ExchangeRateIQ | 45+ | UK-focused corridors, strong GBP corridors | Limited provider coverage |
| CompareRemit | 50+ | India/Pakistan focus, good NRI audience | Outdated UI, weak E-E-A-T |
| FXCompared | 40+ | Business FX focus | Limited retail coverage |
| SaveOnSend | 35+ | Deep corridor data, good analytics | Poor UX, dated design |

### Provider Marketing Pages (Rank for Their Own Brand + Competitors)
Wise, Remitly, OFX, WorldRemit, Western Union — all rank their comparison/marketing pages for "X vs Y" and "send money to [country]" queries. They have massive DR but biased content.

### Our Competitive Advantages
1. **Neutral comparison** — not a provider, no bias (resonates with Google's helpful content update)
2. **Real-time data** — quotes refreshed every 6 hours via scraper pipeline
3. **Breadth** — 35+ providers, 80+ corridors, 189 corridor pages
4. **Depth** — editorial reviews, business hub, SWIFT/IBAN tools, exchange rate tracking
5. **Technical SEO foundation** — schema, hreflang, IndexNow, AI crawler access all set up day 1
6. **3 locales** — EN/ES/FR gives us Spanish and French language long-tail with minimal competition

#### 2.2 Content Template for Enriched Corridors

Each priority corridor page should have:

1. **Hero section** — Live comparison widget (already exists)
2. **"Cheapest way to send money from [A] to [B]"** — H2 with live data table
3. **"How to send money from [A] to [B]"** — Step-by-step guide (3-5 steps)
4. **"[A] to [B] transfer fees compared"** — Fee breakdown table by provider
5. **"How long does it take?"** — Delivery time comparison
6. **"Bank transfer vs online providers"** — Cost comparison showing savings
7. **"Transfer limits"** — Min/max by provider
8. **Regulatory info** — Which providers are licensed in source country
9. **FAQ section** (8-12 questions matching GSC long-tail queries)
10. **Related corridors** — Cross-links to reverse corridor + same-destination from other countries

**Target: 2,500-4,000 words per enriched corridor page**

## 7. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Google algorithm update penalizes thin content | Medium | High | Ensure every page has unique, substantial value (no keyword-stuffing) |
| Provider API changes break scrapers | High | Medium | Multiple data sources per provider, graceful fallbacks |
| Competitor launches similar comparison tool | Medium | Medium | Speed advantage (6hr updates), editorial depth, 3-locale coverage |
| AI Overviews reduce click-through rates | High | High | GEO optimization, brand mentions, YouTube presence |
| Vercel Hobby plan limits | Low | Low | Monitor bandwidth; upgrade if traffic exceeds limits |
| Google honeymoon period ends | Already happened | Low | Normal behavior — rankings stabilize after 60-90 days |
| Business hub content perceived as thin | Low | Medium | Each business page has real provider data, not just keyword variations |
